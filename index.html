Our products

Native Vaults

Built with Bitcoin in mind, it’s a revolutionary product that provides easy-to-use self-custody of digital assets natively.

No wrapped tokens or bridge hacks; you own the actual asset and control them 24/7. We’ve made multi-sig accounts easy to configure and support air-gapped hardware and hot wallets. Native Vaults is the most secure and convenient way to store and manage digital assets.

Hancock Signer

A WASM-based Polkadot wallet designed to make interacting with the Polkadot blockchain easier and more intuitive.

While keeping private keys in the device, it removes UX barriers to entry, making signing up and connecting to dApps frictionless. No browser plugin is required! Scan a QR code with your phone and log in or approve transactions. Hancock Signer is the best daily driver solution in the Polkadot ecosystem.

Fund Administration

Blockchain is the internet of money but to bring this vision to life Hashed Network has ported the protocols used to manage money at scale.

Our solution currently secures the process that moves $1B+ in assets for multiple large real estate projects.
